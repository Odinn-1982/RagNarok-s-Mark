### **1. Smart Auto-Application**
**What it means:** The module figures out which conditions to apply automatically.

**Example:** When you cast a spell that poisons someone, it automatically adds "Poisoned" to them. You don't have to remember.

---

### **2. Two Condition Lists**
**What it means:** Each character has TWO lists of conditions - one active, one on hold.

**Example:** Your character is "concentrating" on a spell. You enter an anti-magic zone, so the module puts "concentrating" on pause. When you leave the zone, it turns back on automatically.

### **3. Chain Reactions**
**What it means:** One condition can trigger another, which triggers another.

**Example:** "Poisoned" triggers "Weakened" which triggers "Movement Slowed". All three happen automatically in order.

### **76. Daggerheart Auto-Defeat System (NEW!)**
**What it means:** Special automation for Daggerheart that handles the "Defeated" vs "Dead" distinction.

**How it works:** When anyone (PC, Adversary, or NPC) reaches 0 HP, they're automatically marked as "Defeated" - NOT dead. You then choose if they're actually dead based on the story.

**Example for PCs:** Your ranger drops to 0 HP. Module marks them "Defeated" automatically. Player decides to spend 2 Hope and pop back up with 5 HP. Module removes "Defeated" automatically. Back in the fight!

**Example for Adversaries:** You drop a goblin to 0 HP. Module marks it "Defeated". You decide narratively: "The goblin is knocked out." Leave it as Defeated. OR: "The goblin dies." Click the "‚ò†Ô∏è Mark as Dead" button on the token.

**Manual Controls:** Three buttons appear on defeated/dead tokens:
- "‚ò†Ô∏è Mark as Dead" - Change Defeated to Dead (narrative choice)
- "üíö Revive (Defeated)" - Bring dead character back to defeated (resurrection prep)
- "‚ú® Fully Revive" - Remove all death conditions (full resurrection)

**Why this matters:** In Daggerheart, being Defeated doesn't mean you're Dead. PCs can recover, enemies can be captured, death is a choice. This system respects that philosophy while automating the tracking.
