## Production Guide

### Equipment Needed

- Screen capture software (OBS Studio - free!)
- Audio recording (built-in or microphone)
- Video editor (DaVinci Resolve - free!)
- Foundry VTT instance

### Settings for Capture

```
Resolution: 1920x1080 @ 60fps
Bitrate: 8000 kbps
Audio: 192 kbps, 48kHz stereo
Color Space: Full Range
```

### Editing Workflow

1. **Capture** - Record raw footage with OBS
2. **Edit** - Cut sections, add transitions in DaVinci
3. **Graphics** - Add titles, highlights, annotations
4. **Audio** - Mix narration, music, sound effects
5. **Export** - 1080p @ 60fps for YouTube

### Music & Audio

**Recommended Royalty-Free Sources:**
- Epidemic Sound
- AudioJungle
- YouTube Audio Library
- FreeMusic Archive

**Audio Levels:**
- Narration: -18dB (primary)
- Music: -30dB (background)
- Effects: -24dB (highlights)
